y:T   
) -> T

plus() is currently typed over all types, as a convenience stopgap until the typeclass/interface functionality comes online.

Results are as follows currently:

  • Int, Long, Float, Double: arithmetic addition
  • Boolean: or
  • String: concatenation
  • List: lplus = concatenation
  • Map: mplus = directional union (right operand wins)
